Burger Recipes

Description

Indulge your taste buds with the irresistible flavors of homemade smash burgers. This simple yet incredibly satisfying recipe begins with premium ground beef, ideally an 80% lean to 20% fat ratio for that perfect balance of juiciness and flavor. Dividing the beef into equally sized portions, each ball is delicately seasoned with salt and pepper before being expertly smashed onto a hot cast-iron skillet. The result is a thin, crispy-edged patty that cooks in just minutes. The magic happens when you flip these patties and, if desired, crown them with a slice of melting cheese for an extra layer of savory goodness. Assemble your burgers on lightly toasted buns, generously adorned with your favorite condiments, and, for added freshness, pile on toppings like crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and zesty pickles. The result is a symphony of textures and tastes â€“ the crunch of the toasted bun, the succulence of the beef, and the burst of flavors from the toppings. This smash burger recipe promises a quick and delicious culinary adventure, perfect for any meal where you crave the comforting simplicity of a perfectly crafted burger.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (80% lean,20% fat)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Optional: Cheese Slices

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet or grill.
  2. Shape ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties on the hot surface, smashing them for a thin, crispy texture.
  4. Flip patties and add cheese if desired, cooking until the desired doneness is achieved.
  5. Toast hamburger buns, assemble burgers with preferred toppings, and serve hot.
